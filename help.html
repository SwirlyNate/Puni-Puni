Soon to be Frequently Asked Questions

What is an unlockable skill?


New to this monster strike collab, NHN has introduced unlockable skills. These skills are completely independant from the Yo-kai's normal skill and is reserved for SSS and above Yo-kai. The Scroll of Honor on the right can be used on Monster Strike Yo-kai to unlock their special skills.

What does this red orb do? / How do I get the new ZZ?


The Red Dragon orb is a crank-a-kai exclusive item that can be fused with Mana S to get Nantoka Mana. Good luck cranking this.

What does this yellow orb do? / How do I get Nantoka Benzaiten?


The Yellow Dragon orb is an ohajiki exclusive item that can be fused with Benzaiten S to get Nantoka Benzaiten. This item can be obtained by clearing all nine super stages of the Ohajiki. I reccomend collaborating with friends to get this done to avoid spending money.
