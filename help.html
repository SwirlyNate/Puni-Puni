Watch Creation - UR Gaia


Watch Fragments


Watch Fragments can be obtained through various missions in the missions menu. These include:

  • Use 1 yokadori ticket
  • Use 5 yokadori tickets
  • Use 10 yokadori tickets
    *Note without spending money on yokadori tickets this mission is un-clearable until the 14th day
  • Get 30 stars on the first event map
  • Get 35 stars on the second event map
  • Get 25 stars on the third event map
  • Clear 3 or more "Combo Challenge" missions
    • Clear stage 2 with 3 or more combos
    • Clear stage 5 with 5 or more combos
    • Clear stage 8 with 7 or more combos
    • Clear stage 13 with 10 or more combos
    • Clear hidden stage 1 with 15 or more combos
  • Clear 3 or more "Score Challenge" missions
    • Clear stage 16 with 150,000 or more score
    • Clear stage 19 with 200,000 or more score
    • Clear stage 23 with 250,000 or more score
    • Clear stage 28 with 300,000 or more score
    • Clear hidden stage 2 with 500,000 or more score
  • Clear 3 or more "Tribe Challenge" missions
    • Clear stage 31 with 1 tribe
    • Clear stage 35 with 1 tribe
    • Clear stage 40 with 1 tribe
    • Clear stage 45 with 1 tribe
    • Clear hidden stage 3 with 1 tribe
  • Beat DG Troopers with 50% or more hp (Stage 46)

Watch Band


The Watch band is a reward for clearing the first seal. Getting all 8 colors of the watch band will unlock this item in the seal menu.

Watch Cover


The watch cover can be obtained from clearing stage 2.

Watch Chip


The watch chip can be obtained from the rare spawn yo-kai. You'll need 20 of these.

Watch Bezel


The watch bezel can be obtained from clearing stage 47. This stage hosts Elzemekia and can be rather hard to beat without T-Dine. Attack boosters are blocked on this stage so go for raw damaging yo-kai.
